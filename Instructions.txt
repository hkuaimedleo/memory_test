Memory Test Game
=============

Link
-----
https://hkuaimedleo.github.io/memory_test/


Instructions
-----
You are given a 5 by 5 grid. When I click the button, some of the tiles will flash in yellow, but only for a short duration.
Your task is to remember the location of the yellow grids and mark them on the paper that is given to you.


Configurations
-----
1. Simple Mode    (For individual students)
- 6 - 7 tiles in yellow
- 0.4 - 0.5 seconds

2. Difficult Mode    (For students working in groups, allow them to discuss a strategy)
- 12 tiles
- 0.1 seconds
